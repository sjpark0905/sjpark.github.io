Hi, I'm Seong-Joon Park, a Staff Engineer at Samsung Electronics. I was a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Institute of Artificial Intelligence at Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH) hosted by Prof. Yongjune Kim, and I will be joining Stony Brook University as a Postdoctoral Researcher under the supervision of Prof. Lav Varshney in September 2026. I received Ph.D. and M.S. degrees in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University (SNU), where I was advised by Prof. Jong-Seon No.

My research area focuses on:
* Information theory, channel coding, and machine learning
* AI/ML-based decoder for error correcting codes
* Quantum error correction & Quantum information theory
* Channel coding for wireless communication and memory systems
